As Chairman and CEO of Mittal Alliance India, I aim to bridge the gap between academia and industry, fostering innovation and empowering leaders. Over the past four years, I've impacted 50,000+ students by providing job placements, internships, and entrepreneurial opportunities.

Committed to youth empowerment, I created a LinkedIn platform that has garnered over 2 million impressions, where I share insights on business strategies, industry trends, and career advice to help young professionals thrive.

Through 150+ seminars and workshops, I've connected students with industry professionals, offering hands-on training and insights. As an author, blogger, podcaster, and speaker, I've created 50+ blogs and podcasts on leadership, industry trends, and success strategies.

Additionally, my consulting work in branding and marketing helps businesses establish strong, impactful identities in today’s competitive landscape. Mittal Alliance has built a reputation as a trusted partner for academia, startups, and MSMEs, with over 75 event participations and numerous testimonials attesting to our impact.

Through initiatives like the Mittal Innovators Network, we’re bridging the gap between education and industry, creating job opportunities, internships, and mentorship sessions that empower emerging professionals. Our strategic partnerships with MSMEs and aspiring entrepreneurs are helping them overcome challenges and accelerate their growth.

Looking forward, my vision is to scale these efforts even further. In the next five years, I aim to empower over 100 million students across India by providing them with the resources, mentorship, and pathways to success in their careers, entrepreneurship, and personal growth. By doing so, I hope to contribute to the creation of a skilled, resilient, and empowered youth ready to lead and drive positive change in society.
